Core Practices

GEO Best
Practices

Five practice areas, each with five actionable best practices. Together they form the complete methodology for positioning content in the AI search layer.

Practice Area 01
Optimize for AI
Search Visibility
  • Implement structured data and Schema Markup to help AI engines parse and surface content accurately
  • Write FAQ-style answers that directly address common queries in the format AI engines prefer to extract
  • Use conversational language that mirrors how users phrase questions to voice assistants and chatbots
  • Optimize for featured snippets by structuring content with clear definitions, lists and step-by-step formats
  • Prioritize factual accuracy with credible sources so AI engines can verify and confidently cite your content
Practice Area 02
Enhance Content for AI
Readability and Summarization
  • Use short paragraphs and bullet points to make content scannable for both AI parsers and human readers
  • Add descriptive subheadings (H2/H3) that clearly signal the topic of each section to AI crawlers
  • Place direct answers at the beginning of sections so AI engines can extract the key point without parsing full paragraphs
  • Include data, statistics and lists that AI engines can reference and incorporate into generated responses
  • Use AI-generated content strategically with human review to maintain quality while scaling production
Practice Area 03
Improve Semantic Search
and Entity Optimization
  • Adopt entity-based SEO by defining and reinforcing key entities, relationships and attributes throughout content
  • Use contextually rich keywords that capture semantic meaning rather than relying on exact-match phrases
  • Build internal linking structures that map content relationships and help AI engines understand topic hierarchies
  • Deploy AI tools for topic clustering to identify content gaps and organize pages into coherent semantic groups
  • Monitor AI search results for keyword refinement and adjust targeting based on how generative engines interpret queries
Practice Area 04
Build Authoritative
and Trustworthy Content
  • Follow E-E-A-T guidelines (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) as the foundation for AI credibility
  • Include author bios and credentials so AI engines can evaluate the authority behind the content
  • Cite reputable sources with proper attribution to build the trust signals AI engines use for ranking
  • Maintain a consistent update schedule to signal freshness and ongoing relevance to AI crawlers
  • Reduce AI-detectable biases by presenting balanced perspectives and disclosing conflicts of interest
Practice Area 05
Optimize for Conversational
AI and Voice Search
  • Write in natural conversational language that matches the way users speak to AI assistants and voice devices
  • Provide concise answers within the first 50 words of each section to maximize voice search extraction
  • Implement Q&A schema to structure content in the question-and-answer format AI engines prefer
  • Optimize local SEO for AI location queries including business listings, geographic terms and "near me" patterns
  • Ensure mobile-friendliness as voice search and conversational AI interactions are predominantly mobile
Decision Framework

Diagnose
and Respond

When performance drops or AI engines bypass your content, use this table to identify the likely cause and the corresponding response.

Symptom Response
Content not appearing in AI search Add structured data, optimize for featured snippets, verify schema implementation
Low engagement from AI responses Improve formatting, use concise direct answers, add bullet points and lists
Inaccurate AI summaries of content Update content clarity, add authoritative links, place key answers at section starts
Weak voice search performance Optimize conversational language, implement Q&A schema, refine local queries
AI prefers competitor content Strengthen authority signals, refresh content, improve E-E-A-T indicators
Continuous Optimization

Reporting and
Iteration

GEO is not a one-time implementation. The AI search landscape shifts as models update, competitors adapt and user behavior evolves. Build a reporting cadence that treats GEO performance as an ongoing operating metric.

  • Monitor AI search trends by tracking which queries trigger generative responses and how your content is represented
  • Track engagement and CTR from AI-generated results to measure whether visibility is converting to meaningful traffic
  • Update content on a regular cadence to maintain freshness signals and correct any AI misrepresentations
  • Use AI tools for performance analysis to identify optimization opportunities and predict shifts in AI search behavior
