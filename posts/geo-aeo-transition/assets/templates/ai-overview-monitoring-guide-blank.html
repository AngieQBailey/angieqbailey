AI Overview Monitoring
& Response Guide

Track generative search presence, analyze impact, respond strategically
Use this template to establish a monitoring cadence for AI Overviews affecting your key search terms. Document which keywords trigger overviews, whether your content appears and what response action to take. Update weekly for high-priority terms and monthly for the full keyword set.

Scenario A: AI Overviews appear but your content is NOT included. Optimize for structured data and E-E-A-T signals.

Scenario B: Competitors dominate AI Overviews. Compare content depth, optimize structure, strengthen off-page authority.

Scenario C: AI Overviews reduce CTR on key pages. Target preview links, shift distribution to LinkedIn/Reddit, monitor long-tail terms.
