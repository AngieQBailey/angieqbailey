Core SEO Principles

The Foundation
Layer

Five principles that underpin every optimization decision. These are non-negotiable regardless of whether the search engine is traditional or AI-driven.

Search Intent Focus
Understanding what the user actually wants when they type a query. Informational, navigational, transactional and commercial intent each require different content structures.
On-Page Optimization
Title tags, meta descriptions, header hierarchy, internal linking and content structure. The basics that still drive 80% of organic visibility.
Technical SEO
Site speed, crawlability, indexation, Core Web Vitals and mobile-first architecture. The infrastructure layer that determines whether content can be found at all.
Content Authority
Depth, accuracy, originality and topical coverage. Search engines and AI models both favor content that demonstrates genuine expertise over surface-level rewrites.
Link Building & E-E-A-T
Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness and Trustworthiness. Backlink quality, author credentials and brand signals that establish credibility with both crawlers and AI systems.
AI Search Optimization

Strategies for
Generative Search

Five strategies that move beyond traditional SEO into the AI search layer. These are the capabilities most teams are missing entirely.

1
Conversational and long-tail keywords. AI search responds to natural language queries. Optimize for how people actually talk, not just how they type.
2
Structured data and schema markup. JSON-LD, FAQ schema and product markup make content machine-readable. AI models pull from structured sources first.
3
Content clusters and semantic SEO. Topical authority through interconnected content hubs. Build depth around subjects rather than chasing isolated keywords.
4
AI-powered content ideation. Use AI tools to identify gaps, generate topic variations and stress-test content angles before production begins.
5
Real-time monitoring and adaptation. Track AI Overview appearances, citation patterns and traffic shifts. Adjust content strategy based on what the AI is actually surfacing.

Workflow

Four Phases,
One Loop

A repeatable four-phase workflow that takes a topic from initial research through continuous optimization. Each phase feeds the next.

Phase 01
Research &
Strategy
Define the audience. Map keyword opportunities across traditional and AI search. Analyze competitors for content gaps, AI Overview presence and citation patterns.
Phase 02
Content Creation
& Optimization
Build content aligned with AI search requirements. Structure for featured snippets and AI summarization. Optimize metadata, headers and internal links for both crawlers and language models.
Phase 03
Technical SEO
& AI Readiness
Ensure mobile-first performance. Implement schema markup across content types. Test AI summary generation and verify structured data renders correctly for generative search.
Phase 04
Performance
Monitoring
Track organic traffic, keyword rankings and AI snippet appearances. Identify what the AI is surfacing vs. ignoring. Make adjustments based on data, not assumptions.
