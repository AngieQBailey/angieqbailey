Crisis Escalation Framework

Medical Center Accreditation Example

This example shows how to classify a real-world crisis according to severity level. The categories below depict a Medical Center's CJCI accreditation trauma. Each level maps to a specific response protocol and a defined team activation.
Level Classification Scenario Response Protocol
Level 1 Critical Loss of accreditation or BLS certification Immediate CEO notification; Activate Crisis Comm Team; Hourly status updates to Board of Directors
Level 2 Significant Compliance finding with imminent survey notice CBO alert; Daily Crisis Comm staff calls; Eternal comm to stakeholders
Level 3 Emerging Corective action plan already in operation; Informal staff concerns IC director alert; Weekly Crisis Comm updates; Corrective action communication to internal staff
Level 4 Operational Raised issue found during onand audits; No patient safety impact QMA department alert; One coll ; Board Notification in agenda
Indicator Key
Red: Critical / Activate Full Crisis Protocol
Yellow: Caution / Aten or Higher / Activate Crisis Team
